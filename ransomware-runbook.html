Introduction

Effective and efficient incident management involves a formal process to detect, analyze, contain, eradicate, recover, and conduct post-incident activities. This runbook provides detailed procedures that govern the incident management process for ransomware attacks.

Incident Management Summary

Question Details
What is the incident? A ransomware attack occurs when malware encrypts organisational systems or data and demands payment for decryption.
Why should we care? Ransomware can halt operations, cause financial losses, damage reputation, and potentially expose sensitive data.
How do we respond? Stakeholders defined in the escalation protocol collaborate across detection, containment, investigation, recovery, and reporting activities.

Response Procedures

Detection Phase

Team Action
End User Report inability to access files or suspicious ransom messages.
Help Desk Create an incident ticket and escalate the issue to cybersecurity.
Cybersecurity Investigate alerts from SIEM, antivirus, or endpoint protection systems.

Analysis Phase

Team Action
Cybersecurity Identify ransomware variant, affected systems, and potential lateral movement.
IT Operations Review system logs to determine entry points and compromised infrastructure.
Legal / HR / PR Evaluate regulatory reporting requirements and potential reputational risks.

Containment Phase

Team Action
Cybersecurity Disable compromised accounts and isolate infected endpoints.
IT Operations Disconnect affected systems from the network and block malicious IP addresses.
CISO Coordinate containment strategy and notify senior management.

Eradication Phase

Team Action
IT Operations Remove malware, patch vulnerabilities, and rebuild compromised systems.
Cybersecurity Perform forensic analysis to confirm attacker removal.
CISO Approve security improvements and mitigation strategies.

Recovery Phase

Team Action
IT Operations Restore data from verified backups and rebuild affected systems.
Cybersecurity Monitor systems to ensure the threat has been eliminated.
Business Units Validate restored systems and resume normal operations.

Post-Incident Phase

Team Action
CISO Conduct post-incident review meetings.
Cybersecurity Document findings and update the incident report.
Legal / HR / PR Update organisational policies and regulatory documentation if required.
